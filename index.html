Frequently Asked Questions

When will my credit card be charged?

Your credit card will be charged AFTER your trip has been accepted by our affiliate. completed. At the time of reservation, we will only authorize your credit card.

How early should I arrive at the airport for my flight?

It is best to ask the airline you are traveling on for their recommendation. Many large airports like LAX tend to have long airport security lines.

What if my arrival flight is delay or arrives early?

All of our affiliates are required to track the arrival flight times. You can also contact your transportation company directly.

How much do you charge if I have to make an extra stop?

All extra stop charges must be arranged and agreed upon by you, the client, and the affiliate.

Do you charge extra for luggage like the airlines?

No we do not. However, it is the customer’s responsibility to order a vehicle that is large enough to accommodate both the passengers and their luggage. If you order a car that is too small, you are still charged for the order you placed.

Can you accommodate large suitcases, skis, or golf bags?

Yes we can. Just be sure to select SUV or van depending on your requirements.

Why do some of the airports have an additional airport fee?

Some airports impose fees for pick-ups from their airport. For instance, LAX charges $4.00 for all pick-ups originating from LAX. There are no fees for drop-offs. This fee is subject to change by airport authorities.

Do you ever have fuel surcharges?

No. You pay whatever appears in your total. There are no hidden fees.

Why are your prices so much lower than the competition?

We have developed systems to take advantage of inefficiencies that may exist in the marketplace so that you can get the best prices..

What is hourly service?

Hourly service is used when you want to have a driver at your disposal to drive you places at your request. It is typically used to go to and from business meetings, for special events such as award ceremonies where you need to have a driver waiting for you, or for special occasions such as an evening out so that you don’t need to be concerned about driving and parking or even having a drink.

How can I contact my driver?

Once your request has been submitted, you will receive all of the contact information for the affiliate company. You can contact the company directly to receive any additional information.

What happens if I have to cancel my trip?

Once your request is accepted, you MAY NOT modify or cancel the trip with our company. It is at the affiliate’s discretion to modify or cancel a trip.
